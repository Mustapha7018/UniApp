About uniAPP

At the heart of every student's higher education journey is the delicate decision-making process. Our platform serves as a guiding light for students in Ghana, offering a centralized hub of university and course information. With our unique geographic-based recommender system and newly introduced academic background matching, we align students with institutions and programs that resonate with their personal strengths and aspirations. We are dedicated to helping each student find their ideal path, making the transition to higher education both thoughtful and inspired.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive University Database: A curated list of universities in Ghana with detailed profiles including courses, eligibility, and contact information.
  • Personalized Recommendations: A geographic-based system that aligns students with universities that best fit their location preferences.
  • Resourceful Guidance: Articles and guides to navigate the higher education landscape, from selecting the right course to understanding application processes.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to upholding the privacy and security of our users, ensuring their data is always protected. Our commitment extends to providing a platform that remains a trustworthy source of current, accurate information, consistently updated to reflect the latest developments, and enhanced through active user engagement and continuous improvements.

Scope of Service

Our platform offers direct links to official university websites for easy access to applications. However, our primary focus is on providing detailed, accurate information and personalized recommendations to guide students in making informed decisions. We do not process applications directly, ensuring our services remain centered on guidance and support.

Our Impact

Our platform has guided over 10,000 students to secure their future by connecting them with their perfect university fit.
